After Hours

The Women in Computing (WiC) After Hours Program is a yearly event for women accepted into the B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing. This Spring it will be a Virtual program from March 26th-28th and April 9th-11th. At After Hours you can get to know about RIT and WiC, as well as meet other accepted and current students. It is organized and hosted by Women in Computing students and in conjunction with the RIT Admission’s Office. It is a great opportunity to meet new friends and possibly your future roommate! This program is BY INVITATION ONLY. Don’t miss this opportunity to get connected to your home away from home! Curious about what it's like as a women in computing at RIT? Check out some of our current Student Stories! Invitations are sent to all female students who have been accepted to RIT's B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences.
